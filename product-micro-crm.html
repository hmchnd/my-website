Introducing Our Micro CRM

Raven Infotech's Micro CRM is designed for small to medium businesses that require a compact, efficient, and effective tool to streamline customer engagement. With its intuitive interface and lightning-fast performance, Micro CRM enables you to:

  • Track customer interactions and history
  • Manage and assign leads efficiently
  • Generate and manage invoices effortlessly
  • Monitor sales pipeline with clear insights
  • Get real-time updates and follow-up reminders

Why Micro CRM?

Unlike bloated enterprise systems, our Micro CRM is lightweight and tailored for speed and simplicity. No complex setups, no steep learning curves — just a powerful tool to manage your customer relationships from a single dashboard.
